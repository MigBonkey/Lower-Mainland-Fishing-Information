Fishing Regulations & Licensing

Stay informed. Fish responsibly.

Why is a License Important?

Licenses help support conservation efforts and ensure sustainable fishing practices for generations to come. It prevents any unnecessary negative interactions with other anglers as well as the Department of Fisheries and Oceans.

How to Obtain a License

  • Visit the official licensing page
  • Follow the instructions for payment and registration

Types of Licenses

Choose from freshwater, saltwater, short-term, or annual licenses depending on your needs.
